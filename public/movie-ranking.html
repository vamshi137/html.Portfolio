My top 3 movies of all time

1. All of Us Are Dead

A thrilling and heartwarming coming-of-age story about a group of people who are separated by grief, living in a small coastal town. This film is an excellent choice for a family viewing party or a movie night.

2. Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is an anime television series based on Chugong's South Korean web novel of the same name.

Golimaar

Golimaar is a 2017 Indian animated film directed by Kiran Kapoor, starring Shobha Ghosh, Kareena Kapoor, and Sukhdev Kapoor. The film is a story of a young man, Raj, who becomes a powerful warrior and a protector of his people in a remote and dangerous region of India.
